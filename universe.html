Quantum Entanglement

Communications

The backbone of interstellar civilization. Entangled particle pairs enable instantaneous communication across any distance, connecting the scattered worlds of humanity in real-time. Omega AIs run their distributed consciousness across quantum-entangled substrates spanning multiple star systems. Without it, sectors would be isolated islands of light, weeks or months apart even at faster-than-light speeds. With it, a message sent from the Gateway Ring reaches Eureka Base before you finish your next breath.

Sector 36

Key Locations

Nova Prime

The capital colony and administrative hub of Sector 36, orbiting Nova Sol. A single mega-city houses 100 million people, with Ingram's largest compute nodes beneath the surface.

Gateway Ring

A ring megastructure near the jump gate, serving as the sector's major port. Once home to 20 million, it's now overwhelmed with 100 million refugees awaiting transport out of the sector. Crime rises as shortages worsen.

Verdant

A lush, green world orbiting the star Demeter. The sector's primary food supplier, once home to 300 million colonists. The exodus has left farms abandoned and harvests uncollected, threatening sector-wide famine.

Forge

An industrial colony on a harsh, metal-rich planet orbiting Vulcan. 250 million colonists live in massive underground cities, working in mining and heavy industry. Corporate security keeps operations running despite the war.

Eureka Base

A research facility on a remote moon orbiting neutron star NS-36-A. Studies the region's gravitational anomalies. Since the war, access is highly restricted and military assets are amassed in the area.

Echo Point

Once a thriving colony of 20 million on a moon in the Venture system. Completely destroyed by Tauran orbital bombardment during the war. The city now lies in ruins‚Äîa stark reminder of first contact gone wrong.

Horizon Station

A frontier outpost on the edge of explored space. Originally a base for further expansion, it now shelters desperate refugees who fled as far from the conflict as possible.

Derelict A1X-VD2

The only Tauran vessel in human space, incapacitated during the war. Adrift in interstellar space and heavily guarded by military forces. Rumors about its cargo and crew abound, but all information is classified.

// Historical Archive

Timeline

32 Years Ago

Sector 36 Jump Gate Opens

Deep-space construction ships sent by Dominion Nexus complete the Sector 36 jump gate, connecting the Viridian Shroud Nebula to the wider interstellar network.

31 Years Ago

Sector 36 Colonization

Colonies are established on Verdant, Forge, and Nova Prime.

Verdant becomes the major farming colony, Forge the industrial hub, and Nova Prime the administrative center.

30 Years Ago

NS-36-A Anomalies Detected

Gravitational anomalies of unknown origin are detected around a neutron star in the sector. Eureka Base is established to study the phenomena.

28 Years Ago

The Gateway Ring Construction Completed

Dominion Nexus completes the construction of the Gateway Ring, a ring structure in proximity to the jump gate, which quickly becomes the logistics hub of the sector.

25 Years Ago

Echo Point Settled

Echo Point is settled as a springboard for sector expansion further towards the core of the galaxy. It ends up becoming the go-to destination for artists around the sector.

5 Years Ago

Titanforge/Abyssal Conflict

Titanforge Industries and the Abyssal Mining Consortium engage in open conflict over astrium deposits on Forge. The AI Council has the military intervene to protect civilian lives. The conflict ends with a merger of the two corporations.

4 Years Ago

Horizon Station Opens

The newest settlement in the sector, Horizon Station is open to the public. Horizon Station is not on a celestial body, rather it is a space habitat orbiting a small rocky planet with no atmosphere.

1 Year Ago

The Tauran War

The Taurans enter Sector 36 and head towards Eureka Base. They use a strange pattern of superluminal flight mixed with stretches of travel at subluminal speed, giving the military a chance to react and prepare defenses.

6 Months Ago

Echo Point Destroyed

The brief Taruan was ends with the devastation of Echo Point. A truce is established soon after and the Taurans withdraw from the sector.

Present Day

Post-War Status

The war and its aftermath have caused major upheaval in Sector 36. Military presence is at an all-time high, the jump gate is used almost exclusively for military traffic, and civilians are desperate to leave the sector after witnessing the destruction of Echo Point.
